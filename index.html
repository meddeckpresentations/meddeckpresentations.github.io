Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MedDeck

How do I get started?

Download MedDeck from the app store or web portal, sign up with your email, and choose from our clinical templates to begin creating.

Can I export to PowerPoint?

Yes! MedDeck supports exporting to PPTX format which is fully compatible with PowerPoint, Keynote, and Google Slides.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and are HIPAA-ready. Your presentations are your own and never shared without permission.

How do I share presentations?

Use our built-in sharing with role-based access control. Share read-only, edit, or comment-only links with specific team members.
